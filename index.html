Hi, I'm Alvin — a 23-year-old video editor and videographer from Kannur, Kerala, India. Over 2 years at Creatox Design Agency, I've worked on branded reels, talking-head content, fashion films, motion graphics, and on-location videography.

Every project gets the same obsession: the right cut at the right moment. I believe editing isn't trimming footage — it's shaping emotion and making the viewer feel something.
